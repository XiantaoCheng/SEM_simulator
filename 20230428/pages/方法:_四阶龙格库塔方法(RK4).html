方法: 四阶龙格库塔方法(RK4)

计算步骤,
$$ x\ _{n+1}=x\ _{n}+\frac{\Delta t}{6}\ \left(a+2\ b+2\ c+d\right) \tag{5} $$ $$ a=f(t\ _{n},x\ _{n}) \tag{6} $$ $$ b=f(t\ _{n}+\Delta t/2,x\ _{n}+\Delta t/2\ a) \tag{7} $$ $$ c=f(t\ _{n}+\Delta t/2,x\ _{n}+\Delta t/2\ b) \tag{8} $$ $$ d=f(t\ _{n}+\Delta t,x\ _{n}+\Delta t\ c) \tag{9} $$ 其中, \(f(t,x)\) 的定义为,
$$ \begin{cases} f_{1}(t,x,y,v_{x},v_{y})=v_{x}\\ f_{2}(t,x,y,v_{x},v_{y})=v_{y}\\ f_{3}(t,x,y,v_{x},v_{y})=\left(v_{y}\ B_{z}(x,y)+E_{x}(x,y)\right)\ q/m\\ f_{4}(t,x,y,v_{x},v_{y})=\left(-v_{x}\ B_{z}(x,y)+E_{y}(x,y)\right)\ q/m \end{cases} \tag{10} $$
